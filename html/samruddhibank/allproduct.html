(SB) Savings Bank Account

Savings Bank Account helps to safeguard your hard-earned money and promote saving habits for a steady and safe future. Saving Bank Account holders can be linked to SMS Banking to add ease in overall transactions carried out by the account holder. We also offer ATM Cards that can be swiped at any sale point. Our Bank offers two types of interest rates on maintaining a balance in the SB account. The interest is calculated on daily products, and the application of interest is quarterly.

Who can Open Account: Any Individual, Group of persons, Society, HUF, Club, and School etc.

How To Open Account: By our prescribed Account opening form.

Nomination Facility: Available. Every account holder should make Nomination to avoid further smooth claiming.
